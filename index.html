Vulcano Candle Holder

Autumn 2023
Candle Holder, Jasmonate

In the combination of candles and candlesticks, candles always take center stage and attract people's attention. This project is a series of volcano-shaped candlesticks that allow melted wax to accumulate on the candlestick over time, just like lava on a volcano, creating a unique candlestick for each person.

Stockwell

Spring 2024
Demonstration Table, Wood

In 2024, Jerry Meng co-designed with residents of the Stockwell community to design a set of multifunctional presentation tables for their newly constructed community art center. The trapezoidal table is moveable and can be pulled from the wall to provide a demonstration for up to 6 people when an event is held. And the storage unit would be accessible at the same time, which is designed to store the sewing machines and tools for the activities.

Squeezeee

Spring 2025
Soft Plastic Compressor, PLA filament, 3D printing

Soft plastic waste cannot be processed in most recycling centers, and it takes a lot of space in bins because it expands. Squeezeee is a 3D printed soft plastic compressor which could be installed on bins and drawers to decrease the space soft plastic waste taken. With a website which could help users customize their own compressor with perfect size to fit different usage and provide 3D file for downloading and printing.
